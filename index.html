Mission

Make student renting in Gaborone fair, documented, and searchable.

Students often rely on scattered WhatsApp posts, rushed viewings, and verbal promises. Nimbus-Habor turns that messy process into a guided platform with listing checks, transparent deposits, in-app messages, digital receipts, landlord profiles, and student reviews.

The goal is simple: help students focus on school instead of chasing refunds, wondering if a listing is real, or negotiating every rental issue alone.

Clear rules before a student pays.

Questions students ask

Clear answers before you sign anything.

Will Nimbus-Habor hold landlords accountable?

Nimbus-Habor is designed to track messages, receipts, reviews, and policy violations. Repeated unfair behaviour can reduce visibility or lead to removal from the platform.

Can students earn points?

Yes. The planned points system rewards completed stays, helpful reviews, and positive platform behaviour.

Will AI features be included?

Yes. Nimbus-Habor is being shaped for AI matching by budget preference, campus, commute, safety needs, and preferred living style.
