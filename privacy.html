Privacy Policy

Nimbus-Habor uses student-first data practices for accommodation search, waitlist, landlord messaging, safety, receipts, and service improvement.

How we handle information

Nimbus-Habor will collect only the information needed to help students find accommodation, message landlords, keep receipts, prevent abuse, and improve safety.

Personal information should not be sold. Sensitive records should be protected with appropriate access controls, and waitlist details should be used to contact people about early access, launch areas, and product feedback.

As product features mature, this policy should be expanded with specific data retention, deletion, analytics, and account-control details.
